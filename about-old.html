About Sankar Motors

Sankar Motors is a legacy of trust and quality spanning over 50 years. Founded by Pendela Peraiah, our shop has been the go-to destination for genuine Bajaj spare parts in Guntur.

We believe in quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Every product we sell is carefully selected to ensure it meets the highest standards.

Our Journey

1974 - Founded

Sankar Motors was established with a vision to provide genuine Bajaj spare parts at fair prices. Starting from a small shop, we served the local community with dedication.

1990s - Growth

As Bajaj motorcycles became more popular, we expanded our inventory and became a trusted supplier for the entire region.

2010s - Recognition

We were recognized as one of the most reliable spare parts dealers in Guntur with 5-star customer ratings.

2024 - Digital Era

We embraced digital transformation and now offer online ordering through WhatsApp for maximum convenience.
